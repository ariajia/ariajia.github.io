Hello,
I'm Aria Jia,

I’m an undergraduate student majoring in Information Science with a minor in Creative Technology and Arts at the University of Colorado Boulder. I gained most of my skills and experience in my course projects. I’ve done several semester-long and data-related projects. The work I’ve done on these projects are writing Python script for data analysis and data visualization, conducting UX research and UX design to improve user experience.

I enjoy the analytic process of turning data into information. Especially when I am working on a practical project, this feeling will be more intense. I really like the feeling of cleaning, filtering, and organizing the data step by step, no matter qualitative or quantitative. These seemingly mundane steps make the data more than just numbers.
