Introduction

The dataset I used came from the INFO 3402 called the 'fandango_score_comparison.csv'.This csv stored every film that has a Rotten Tomatoes rating, a RT User rating, a Metacritic score, a Metacritic User score, and IMDb score, and at least 30 fan reviews on Fandango. The data from Fandango was pulled on Aug. 24, 2015.

Version 1

I used Altair to do the visualization for this assignment because I am more familiar with Altair. The x-axis is the Rotten Tomato Tomatometer score for the film, and the y-axis is the The Rotten Tomatoes user score for the film. The below is my first version chart.

Version 2 & Improvment

I improved my chart by adding the legend of the film name, as well as the colors. And I also make the circle size vary with the number of Fandango votes number.

Version 3 & Improvment

For the last version, I made my chart interactive. You can hover your mouse on each dot, and then you will see the film name as well as the fandango votes count. You can also scoll the chart to make it zoom in or zoom out.
